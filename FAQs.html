1. Who is Professor Bradley?

Professor Faith Bradley is a faculty member at George Washington University, specializing in machine learning within the Columbian College of Arts & Sciences. She is actively involved in teaching and advancing data analytics to support global decision-making. Her work often focuses on translating complex data into practical tools that can help address broad societal challenges, aligning with GW's emphasis on interdisciplinary solutions for real-world issues.


2. Why is there a fan* for Professor Bradley?

This web application exist because some educators leave a lasting impact on their students, inspiring admiration and a sense of community around their personalities, teaching styles, and expertise. Often, students feel a deep connection with professors who are particularly engaging, funny, or passionate about their subjects, which can lead to fan pages as a way to celebrate and share experiences. These pages might feature memorable quotes, classroom anecdotes, or even humorous content inspired by the professor's unique approach to teaching. Professor Bradley is one of those professors!
